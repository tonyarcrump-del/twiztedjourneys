Using a Pause Spinner?

Leave a quick check-in. These spinners are here as a simple way to help yourself pause, breathe, and reset for a moment. You don’t have to do anything with this form — but if you want to leave a note, we’d love to hear from you.

Quick Check-In

Scan After Your Pause

All fields are optional except your name or nickname. This is not a medical form — just a way for you to record a moment.

A quick note on privacy: Please do not share medical details or anything you would not want others to see. These check-ins are reviewed privately by Twizted Journeys staff only. They are not shared publicly, used for clinical purposes, or connected to any treatment record.

Twizted Journeys is not a clinical service, therapy provider, or crisis hotline. This form is not part of any treatment or diagnosis. If you need immediate help, call or text 988 (US, free, 24/7).

Twizted Journeys is not a crisis hotline. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call emergency services now.
In the US: call or text 988 or visit 988lifeline.org. Text HOME to 741741.

Light Up the Night 2026

Secure Your Vendor Space

Vendor registration and payment are handled through our Donorbox page. Spaces are limited — register early to guarantee your spot.

📅 September 12–13, 2026
🏢 40 & 8 Building
500 Frank Street, Shelbyville, IN 46176
🏵 Vendors welcome both days — $50 / day
Register as a Vendor Questions? Email Us

Important: Please include “Vendor Registration — Light Up the Night” in the note or comment field on the Donorbox page so we can identify your registration.

ⓘ Registration is not complete until payment has been submitted through Donorbox. After completing payment you will receive a confirmation. If you do not receive one within 24 hours, email info@twiztedjourneys.org.

Vendor Information

What You Need to Know

🏠 Who Should Register

  • Local artisans & crafters
  • Food vendors & concessions
  • Nonprofits & awareness organizations
  • Businesses supporting recovery & community
  • Anyone with products or services to share with the community

🎉 What Registration Covers

Registration is $50 per day. Register for one or both days through the Donorbox page.

  • One vendor space per day registered
  • Access to the 40 & 8 Building grounds
  • Participation in a community-first awareness event

📦 What to Bring

  • Your own table, chairs, and tent or canopy
  • All products, displays, and signage
  • Extension cords if power is needed (confirm availability in advance)
  • Your own payment processing setup
  • Confirmation of your Donorbox registration

📞 Questions & Contact

Have questions about vendor spaces, setup, or logistics? We’re real people and we always respond.

✉ info@twiztedjourneys.org

You can also reach us through the contact form on the Events page — select “Vendor” as your registration type.

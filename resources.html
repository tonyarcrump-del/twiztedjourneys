Resources & Support

Twizted Journeys is not a crisis hotline, counseling center, or emergency service. These organizations provide professional mental health support, crisis services, and resources.

Organizations That Can Help

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential crisis support 24/7. Call or text 988 from anywhere in the US.

988lifeline.org →

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to reach a trained crisis counselor — free, 24/7.

crisistextline.org →

AFSP — American Foundation for Suicide Prevention

Research, advocacy, and survivor resources. Twizted Journeys is a proud AFSP advocate.

afsp.org →

NAMI — National Alliance on Mental Illness

The nation's largest grassroots mental health organization. Free helpline and local resources.

nami.org →

Overdose Lifeline Indiana

Indiana-specific overdose prevention, support, and resources for families.

overdoselifeline.org →

Indiana Suicide Prevention Coalition

Statewide coalition working toward a suicide-safer Indiana. Twizted Journeys is a member.

indianasuicideprevention.org →

Peer Support

Looking for Peer Support?

Finding Forward meets every Tuesday evening in Shelbyville, Indiana. Free, no cost, adults 18+.

Email for Details →

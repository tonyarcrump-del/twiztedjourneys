What This Means

Every Journey Is Twizted

A pause is not just one thing. It can be grief, loss, or the weight of loving someone who is gone. It can be a mental health struggle, an addiction journey, a traumatic chapter, a season of rebuilding, or a moment when everything had to stop before anything could continue.

Twizted Journeys was founded by Tonya Crump and Tia Weaver in Shelbyville, Indiana after experiencing profound personal loss. They turned that loss into a movement — a community that holds space for anyone who is still here, still walking, and still finding their way forward.

We are not here to diagnose, treat, or counsel. We are here to show up alongside you. Through awareness events, peer support, memorials, storytelling, and community connection — we remind each other that continuing after the pause is worth it.

Twizted Journeys, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit rooted in Shelbyville, Indiana and reaching people through awareness, remembrance, resources, and community connection. Programs are open to the community.

How We Show Up

Community. Awareness. Hope.

💜

Finding Forward

Twizted Journeys’ peer-led space specifically for suicide loss survivors — those who are continuing after losing someone to suicide. Free, every Tuesday evening in Shelbyville. Adults 18+.

🌟

S.A.F.E. Events

Suicide Awareness For Everyone — awareness rides, community walks, and gatherings that bring people together around hope, remembrance, and connection. Open to everyone. Come as you are.

🕊️

Memorial & Stories

Honor someone you’ve lost. Share your own journey in a space that holds your story with care and zero judgment.

🏭

Community Events

Vendor tables, fundraisers, and shoe drives — we show up in the community year-round to build connection and keep awareness of grief, mental health, and hope in front of people.

Stay With Us

Keep Walking Your Journey

Your journey belongs here — wherever it has taken you, whatever pause you are carrying. Whether you want to stay connected, honor someone you’ve lost, or just know this community exists — we’re here.

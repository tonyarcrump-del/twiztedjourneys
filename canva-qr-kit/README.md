## Canva Usage Notes

### Uploading
- In Canva, click **Uploads** → **Upload files** → select the PNG files from this folder.
- All files are 600×600 px PNG, suitable for print and digital use.

### Placing in a design
- Do **not** crop into the QR code itself — always keep the full white border (quiet zone) visible around all four sides.
- Do **not** stretch, skew, or rotate the QR code. Keep it square.
- Do **not** place the QR over a dark, patterned, or busy background. Use a white or very light solid background behind it.
- If your design has a dark background, add a white rectangle behind the QR in Canva before placing the code on top.

### Sizing
- **Flyers and signs:** minimum 1.5 inches square. 2 inches or larger is better.
- **Business cards:** minimum 1 inch square if space allows.
- **Shirts / DTF transfers:** minimum 2.5 inches wide; 3 inches recommended for chest or back placement. Never place on a seam or pocket edge.
- **Small merch tags (charms, stones):** minimum 0.75 inches; laminate for durability.

### Labels
Always add a short text label under (or near) the QR code so people know what they are scanning. Suggested labels:

| QR Code | Suggested Label Text |
|---|---|
| Events QR | Scan for Upcoming Events |
| Shoe Drive QR | Scan for Shoe Drive Info |
| Continue After the Pause QR | Scan to Follow the Journey |
| Twizted Treasure Tracker QR | Scan to Continue This Treasure's Journey |
| Pause Spinner Check-In QR | Scan After Your Pause |

### Testing
- **Always test-scan** with both an iPhone camera and an Android camera before sending to print or ordering transfers.
- If the scan fails, the QR may be too small, too low-contrast, or distorted. Fix in Canva before printing.
- For DTF shirt transfers: print one test transfer and scan the shirt before committing to a full run.

## DTF Shirt Transfer Notes — Continue After the Pause

**Recommended shirt layout:**
```
Continue After the Pause
[QR CODE — min 2.5 inches wide]
Scan to follow the journey.
twiztedjourneys.org
```

**Print specs:**
- QR minimum: 2.5 inches wide (3 inches recommended for chest/back)
- Contrast: black QR on white or very light background only
- If shirt is dark: add a white rectangle base behind the QR block in Canva
- Do NOT stretch, skew, or rotate the QR
- Test scan before ordering the full run

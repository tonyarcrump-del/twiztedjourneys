📷 Canva Usage Notes

Uploading

  • Click Uploads in Canva
  • Select Upload files
  • Choose the PNG files from this folder
  • All files are 600×600 px — print-ready

Placing & Sizing

  • Keep the full white border around the QR
  • Do not crop into the code
  • Flyers/signs: min 1.5″ square
  • Business cards: min 1″ square
  • Shirts/DTF: min 2.5″ wide (3″ preferred)
  • Small tags/charms: min 0.75″

Backgrounds

  • Do not distort or rotate the QR
  • Use black QR on white or light background
  • On dark designs: add a white rectangle behind the QR in Canva first
  • Never place over a pattern or busy background

Before Printing

  • Test-scan with iPhone camera
  • Test-scan with Android camera
  • For DTF: print one test transfer and scan before ordering the full run
  • If scan fails: make the QR larger

Who We Are

A free, open community for anyone carrying grief, loss, or the weight of life’s hardest chapters — in Shelby County and beyond.

Built by Loss. Driven by Hope.

Twizted Journeys is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit founded in Shelbyville, Indiana by Tonya Crump and Tia Weaver — two women who turned personal loss into a lifeline for their community. We exist to make sure no one has to carry the weight of grief, suicide loss, or mental health struggle alone. Every program we offer is completely free. No membership, no cost, ever.

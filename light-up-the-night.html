Community Awareness Weekend

September 12–13, 2026

Join Twizted Journeys for an awareness ride, community walk, vendors, remembrance, and connection. Riders, walkers, families, supporters, and community members are welcome.

  • Location 40 & 8 Building, 500 Frank Street, Shelbyville, Indiana
  • Saturday, September 12 Light Up the Night awareness ride. Gates open at 11 AM; kickstands up at 12:30 PM.
  • Sunday, September 13 The Cause awareness walk. Gates open at 11 AM; walk starts at 1:30 PM.

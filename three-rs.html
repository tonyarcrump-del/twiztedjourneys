3 R's

A New US!!!

Here we help others Reunite, Reinvest, and Reinvent themselves.

Topics here at times will have:
Deep meaning  ·  Hard Honesty  ·  Lite Laughter  ·  Dark Sarcasm

Please feel free to submit something of your own with the form at the bottom of the page. If approved we will inform you and send you a link to be shared so everyone can see your submission online.

1

Reunite

Reconnect with yourself, your values, your people. Sometimes healing starts with finding your way back to who you were before the pain.

2

Reinvest

Put energy back into the things that matter. Invest in your mental health, your relationships, your community, and your future.

3

Reinvent

You don't have to stay who the hard years made you. Growth is possible. A new chapter is possible. A new you is possible.

What is Rule 62 in recovery?

Rule 62 comes from the rules and regulations that were determined when the Big Book of Alcoholics Anonymous was created to try and inspire the people in recovery to have common values, beliefs and lifestyles that they were working towards.

"Don't take life too seriously."

— Rule 62

🤝 The 3 R's in Action — Finding Forward

Our Tuesday evening peer support group, Finding Forward, lives the 3 R's every week. Loss survivors, mental health fighters, and community members come together to reconnect, reinvest in healing, and reinvent their path forward — together.

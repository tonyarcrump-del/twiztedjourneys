Our Story

About Twizted Journeys

In 2021, Tonya's nephew Jason decided his story didn't need to be written anymore. That loss was devastating — it changed what "normal" means for our family forever. It also made Tonya start thinking about her own past, and even her son. We have both been where Jason was. That dark cloud is very hard to crawl out from underneath.

Tonya reached out blindly, not knowing where to turn, and found the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention. Since August 2021, she and her daughter have become advocates for AFSP, NAMI, and Overdose Lifeline.

With the help of her daughter, Tonya founded "Jason's Journey" — to keep his story going and to help raise awareness for others who are suffering. We quickly realized it wasn't just Jason who had a twizted journey. Everyone has a journey, and every journey is twizted in its own way. So began Twizted Journeys.

Twizted Journeys, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit public charity. Donations are tax-deductible as allowed by law. We are proud advocates with AFSP and proud members of the Indiana Suicide Prevention Coalition.

Tonya Crump
Founder & CEO

Our commitment: Twizted Journeys is a safe, welcoming space for people carrying grief, loss, mental health struggles, addiction recovery stories, suicide loss, and the weight of life's hardest chapters. You do not have to be okay to be here. Every journey matters here.

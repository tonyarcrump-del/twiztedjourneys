- Volunteer / Partner: https://twiztedjourneys.org/about.html
- Merch: https://twiztedjourneys.org/merch.html
- Resources: https://twiztedjourneys.org/resources.html

Keep QR codes named by destination, stored in `images/qr/`, and tested after every destination update.

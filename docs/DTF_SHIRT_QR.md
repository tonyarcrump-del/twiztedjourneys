## Print Notes

- **QR size:** Minimum 2.5 inches wide. Recommended 3 inches for chest/back placement.
- **Contrast:** Use high contrast — black QR on white or very light background. Avoid printing QR over dark fabric, patterns, or photos.
- **Placement:** Center chest or upper back. Never place QR on a curved seam or pocket edge.
- **Background:** If shirt is dark, use a white rectangle base beneath the QR block.
- **Distortion:** Do NOT stretch, skew, or rotate the QR. Square and level always.
- **Test first:** Scan the `images/qr/qr-continue-after-pause.png` file with iOS camera, Android camera, and a dedicated QR app before sending to print.
- **Order small first:** Print one test transfer and scan the shirt before committing to a full run.
- **Resolution:** Use the 600×600 PNG for digital proofing. Request 1200×1200+ from your DTF supplier if they require hi-res.

## About the QR Destination

`continue.html` is a warm, mission-safe landing page that:
- Explains "Continue After the Pause" in plain, nonprofit-appropriate language
- Links to Home, Events, Donate, Resources, Share Your Journey, and Twizted Treasures
- Includes 988 crisis support language (footer crisis band + disclaimer)
- Does NOT claim to provide therapy, treatment, diagnosis, emergency response, or crisis counseling

The page is designed to work for anyone who scans — whether they're a supporter, a grieving family member, someone in a hard season, or a curious stranger.

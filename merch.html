🤲 Handmade by Tonya

New

Each handmade piece Tonya creates carries its own story — and now, it carries yours too.

Every handmade item — bracelets, keychains, cups, memorial pieces — gets its own unique ID and a printed QR code tag. When you scan the tag with your phone, you can share where the piece found you, what it means to you, or who it reminds you of.

Your story is private by default. Tonya reviews each story personally. You choose whether it's shared publicly, shared anonymously, or kept just between you and her.

Each handmade item includes:

  • ✦ Unique item ID (TJ-HM-0001 format)
  • ✦ Printed QR code tag
  • ✦ Private story form — yours to fill or leave blank
  • ✦ Option to share your story publicly, anonymously, or keep it private
  • ✦ 988 crisis line always visible on the form

📱 Scan your item's QR tag

If you have a handmade Twizted Journeys item, scan the QR code on the tag to share your story.

How It Works
  1. 1. Scan the QR tag on your handmade item.
  2. 2. The story form opens on your phone.
  3. 3. Share what you'd like — or nothing at all.
  4. 4. Your story is private until you say otherwise.
  5. 5. Tonya reads each story personally.

📷 DTF Shirt Transfer Notes — Continue After the Pause
Recommended Shirt Layout
Continue After the Pause [QR CODE — min 2.5 inches wide] Scan to follow the journey. twiztedjourneys.org
Print SpecRequirement
QR minimum size2.5 inches wide (3 inches recommended for chest/back)
ContrastBlack QR on white or very light background only
BackgroundUse a white rectangle base if shirt is dark
PlacementCenter chest or upper back. Never on a seam or pocket edge.
DistortionDo NOT stretch, skew, or rotate the QR
TestScan with iOS camera, Android camera, and a QR app before ordering
OrderPrint one test transfer and scan the shirt before committing to a full run

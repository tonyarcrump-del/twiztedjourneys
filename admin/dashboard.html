ⓘ About Form Submissions

Memorial submissions, story submissions, and event RSVPs are collected by Netlify Forms and sent to tonyarcrump@gmail.com automatically. They also appear in your Netlify dashboard under Forms. Once Supabase is connected, all new submissions will also be stored in the database and appear in the Submissions panel above with approval/reject controls.

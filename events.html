Our History

Where We’ve Been

Since 2021, Twizted Journeys has hosted awareness rides, community walks, vendor events, and peer gatherings locally and beyond. Every year has brought more community, more healing, and more hope.

2025 — Light Up the Night

Our 2025 awareness event brought together riders, walkers, vendors, and community members for a powerful night of awareness, connection, and hope.

2022 — A Journey to Be Stigma Free

A landmark two-day event — our second annual benefit ride and first annual awareness walk. The community showed up in force.

2021 — The Beginning

Driven by personal loss, Tonya launched what would become Twizted Journeys — a community built on shared experience, mutual support, and the belief that every journey matters.

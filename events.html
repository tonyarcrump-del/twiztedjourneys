Past Events

"Light Up the Night" hosted by Twizted Journeys

Our 2025 Light Up the Night event brought together riders, walkers, and community members for a powerful night of awareness. Volunteers including Katrina and Tina helped make the event possible — and their memory is now part of the Twizted Journeys family forever.

Past Events

2022 brought about "A Journey to be Stigma Free" — this was a huge 2-day event that had our second annual benefit ride and our 1st annual awareness walk.

2021

Past Events

2021 was the year it all began. Driven by personal loss, Tonya and her daughter launched what became Twizted Journeys — starting with awareness events and the first peer connections.

How It Works

Every Treasure Has a Journey

Twizted Treasure items are handmade pieces that carry meaning wherever they go. When someone receives or finds one, they can share its story.

1

Receive or Find It

Someone gives you a Twizted Treasure — a charm, bracelet, or keepsake made by Tonya — or you come across one in the world.

2

Scan the QR Code

Each item comes with a small QR tag. Scan it with your phone camera and you’ll land right here — on this page.

3

Share the Journey

Tell us where it traveled, who it honors, or what it meant to you. You choose how much or how little to share.

4

Keep, Gift, or Pass Forward

Hold on to it. Give it to someone who needs the message today. Or set it somewhere for the next person to find.

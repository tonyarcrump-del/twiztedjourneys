The Twizted Journeys Podcast

Real stories of grief, loss, mental health, healing, and hope — told by the people who lived them. We're building this together with our community.

Content Disclosure — Please Read

Twizted Journeys shares personal stories for awareness, connection, and hope. Some stories may include grief, suicide loss, addiction, overdose, trauma, or mental health struggles. These stories are not therapy, medical advice, crisis counseling, or emergency support.

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call emergency services now. In the United States, call or text 988 or visit 988lifeline.org for crisis support.

If you are under 18: Please have a parent or guardian help you before submitting a story, appearing on the podcast, or sharing personal details publicly. We want your voice heard safely.

Interested in sharing your story on the podcast? Reach out and we'll walk you through the process together.

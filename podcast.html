About the show

Where silence becomes a signal

Hope Signal is a podcast about the journeys we don't talk about enough — suicide loss, mental health struggles, recovery, grief, and the quiet courage it takes to keep showing up. Every episode is a real conversation with a real person who has lived through something hard and found their way to the other side.

We don't whisper here. We don't soften the edges. We tell the truth — because the truth is what sets people free from the shame that too often silences them.

Whether you're a loss survivor, a mental health advocate, someone who's struggled yourself, or someone who loves a person who has — this podcast is for you.

Be a guest

Your story matters

Content Disclosure
Hope Signal shares personal stories for awareness, connection, and hope. Episodes may include discussions of grief, suicide loss, addiction, overdose, trauma, and mental health challenges. This podcast is not therapy, medical advice, crisis counseling, or emergency support. If you or someone you know is in crisis, please call or text 988, text HOME to 741741, or call 911.
If you are under 18
Please have a parent or guardian present before appearing on the podcast, submitting a personal story, or sharing private details. We want your voice heard safely.
